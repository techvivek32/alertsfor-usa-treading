## 1. What is TradeScope?

TradeScope watches the entire US stock market for you — **live, every second** — and does the hard work of spotting the best opportunities so you don't have to stare at screens all day.

Think of it as a **smart assistant** that:

- 👀 Tracks the big US markets and dozens of top stocks in real time
- 🧠 Reads the **latest news** and weighs it together with price trends
- 🏆 Tells you the **single best opportunity** at any moment — and exactly what to do
- 🛡️ Shows you **where to take profit** and **where to exit** so you're never caught off guard

You stay in control. TradeScope simply hands you clear, ready-to-act information.

## 3. The Five Sections — Explained Simply

### 🏆 Best Trade of the Moment
This is the **headline** — the one opportunity our system rates highest right now, after weighing price trends, market activity, and the latest news together.

It gives you, in plain words:
- **The stock** to focus on
- **A short reason** why it stands out today
- **What to do** (the exact buy / sell / exit prices)
- **The main risk** to keep in mind

👉 *If you only look at one thing, look here.*

### 📈 Live Index Charts
The four biggest US markets (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones, Russell 2000), shown as live moving charts. **Green = going up, red = going down.** This gives you the overall "mood" of the market in one glance.

### 💹 Live Prices
A live board of top US stocks. Each price updates **every second**, and a tile flashes **green or red** the moment a trade happens — so you can feel the market breathing in real time.

### 📊 Strategy Reality Check
This is our **honesty section** — and it's what sets a professional tool apart. It shows how the strategy has actually performed when tested on **400 days of real market history**: the win rate, the typical gain vs. loss, and the worst rough patch.

We show you the real numbers — the good and the uncomfortable — so you can make decisions with your eyes open.

### 🔔 Buy Alerts
Up to **five** of the strongest opportunities right now. Each alert is a complete, ready-to-act plan (see Section 6 for how to read one).

## 4. How Often Does It Update?

Everything refreshes automatically — you never need to do anything.

| What | How often it updates |
|------|----------------------|
| 💹 Live prices & charts | **Every 1 second** (real-time) |
| 🔔 Buy Alerts & 🏆 Best Trade | **Every 10 minutes** — the system re-scans the whole market and keeps only the best opportunities. If a stronger one appears, it automatically replaces a weaker one. |
| 📊 Strategy track record | Refreshed a few times a day |

So your prices are always *live*, and your alerts are always *fresh* — never more than 10 minutes old.

> 💡 A small line under the alerts shows the last update time and a countdown to the next one, so you always know how current the information is.

## 5. When Is TradeScope Active?

TradeScope follows the **US stock market hours**. In Indian time, that is roughly:

🕖 **7:00 PM to 1:30 AM IST**, Monday to Friday.
*(This shifts by about an hour during the US winter, becoming ~8:00 PM to 2:30 AM IST.)*

During these hours, everything is live and the smart alerts are fully active.

**Outside market hours** (and on weekends), the markets are closed worldwide — so the dashboard shows the **last available information** and pauses the smart analysis. This is intentional: it keeps everything accurate and efficient. Live action simply resumes automatically when the US market reopens.

## 6. How to Read & Use an Alert

Every alert is a **complete plan**. Here's what the three key numbers mean:

| Label | Meaning | Colour |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 🔵 **Entry** | The price to **buy** at | Blue |
| 🟢 **Target** | The price to **sell** at, to book your profit | Green |
| 🔴 **Stop** | The price to **exit** at, to limit your loss if it goes wrong | Red |

You'll also see a **"Now"** price that updates live, so you can watch how the trade is doing against your plan.

### 🛡️ The one golden rule (please read this)

Professional traders don't win every trade — and neither will any tool. They stay profitable by following **strict risk control**. The single most important habit:

> **Never risk more than 1% of your money on a single trade.**

That way, even a string of losing trades can't seriously hurt you — and you stay in the game long enough for the winners to do their work. The **Stop** price in every alert is there precisely to help you do this.

## 7. Important: What This Is — and What It Isn't

We believe in being straight with you.

✅ **What TradeScope is:** a powerful, real-time decision-support tool that combines market data, technical signals, and AI-read news to surface high-quality opportunities — far faster and more consistently than doing it by hand.

⚠️ **What it is not:** a guarantee of profit. **No honest tool, anywhere, can promise that.** Markets are uncertain. As the Strategy Reality Check shows, a meaningful share of signals will not work out — and that is completely normal. The strategy aims to profit over time by **keeping losses small and letting winners run larger**, combined with the 1% rule above.

Use TradeScope to **inform** your decisions, never to replace your own judgement. All trading carries real risk of financial loss. Please trade responsibly and only with money you can afford to put at risk.
